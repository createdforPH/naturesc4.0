ABOUT US

Nature SC is Nature Standard Care

With the tagline “Skin Wears Nature”, our company’s direction is about specializing in skincare products with natural ingredients and scientific expertise. Significantly, our collaboration with GK Medical Group has helped us to achieve the distinctive formulas that our products have. Originally, our company belonged to Taesung Planet but especially from this year, we have been acquired by DW Group International. So we are more dedicated to give you even better results than before. We assure you that we have a meticulous quality control so every one of our products is able to keep excellent standards.   

GK Medical Group GK Medical Group is doing cosmetic procedures to celebrities, politician and many other famous people, has plastic surgery, dermatology, anti-aging center, stem cell center, medical examination center, and semi-permanent makeup and processing a research for various natural product groups, as the first medical group in the country, with DW Group International.  

Coming to the Philippines! From March of 2018, NatureSC Philippines was born. The old products of NatureSC have actually been in the Philippines for a few years but there was no one who was able to establish our brand in this country. With NatureSC officially recognizing its Country Manager in the Philippines, our company is now becoming more recognized. In addition, our team of resellers is becoming bigger as well as our new and improved products are amazingly reaching more of our fellow Filipinos all over the country - from Luzon, Visayas, and to Mindanao. We are making a skincare sensation in this country and how great it is if you would be part of this growth!
